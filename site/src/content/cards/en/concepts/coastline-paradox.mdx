<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>A coastline has no definite length. As the measuring unit shrinks, the measured total length grows — in theory without bound. Zoom into a straight ruler and it stays straight; zoom into a coastline and new bays and capes appear. That is the essential difference between rectifiable curves and fractal curves.</CoreDef>
</Band>
