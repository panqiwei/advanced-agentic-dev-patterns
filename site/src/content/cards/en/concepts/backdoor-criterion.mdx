<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>A set S satisfies the backdoor criterion if: ① no node in S is a descendant of X; ② S blocks every "backdoor path" from X to Y (any path ending with an arrow into X). When it holds, the backdoor adjustment formula applies.</CoreDef>
</Band>
