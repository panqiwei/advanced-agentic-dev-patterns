  <h2 class="card-title">Heuristic Search</h2>
  <p class="card-subtitle">Heuristic Search — generate-and-test framework · search volume ≠ intelligence</p>
</Band>

<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>The solution to a problem is represented as a symbol structure; you generate and incrementally modify symbol structures until one satisfies the goal. "Heuristic" means: not exhaustive, not random, but selective generation guided by information. Knowing how to test a solution is not the same as knowing how to generate one.</CoreDef>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-raised" grow>
  <div class="explosion-note">
    <span class="en-formula">{"B = 10, D = 10 → 10¹⁰ candidates  |  chess B≈35, D≈40 → astronomical"}</span>
    <span class="en-note">The fundamental bottleneck of brute force: finite compute; serial search must be selective</span>
  </div>
  <div class="methods-grid">
    <Kicker>Weak methods vs strong methods</Kicker>
    <div class="mg-row weak-header"><span class="mg-cat">WEAK</span><span class="mg-desc">When domain knowledge is thin, general heuristics tame exponential blow-up</span></div>
    <div class="mg-row"><span class="mg-method">Means-ends analysis</span><span class="mg-desc">Detect gap → pick an action that shrinks it (GPS 1957)</span></div>
    <div class="mg-row"><span class="mg-method">Best-first search</span><span class="mg-desc">Expand the node closest to the goal next</span></div>
    <div class="mg-row"><span class="mg-method">Alpha-beta pruning</span><span class="mg-desc">Prune branches that cannot change the final evaluation</span></div>
    <div class="mg-row strong-header"><span class="mg-cat">STRONG</span><span class="mg-desc">Rich domain knowledge reduces or eliminates search</span></div>
    <div class="mg-row"><span class="mg-method">Semantic recognition</span><span class="mg-desc">Chess masters store 50,000+ board patterns and recognize directly</span></div>
    <div class="mg-row"><span class="mg-method">Representation change</span><span class="mg-desc">Mutilated-chessboard problem → color symmetry makes the answer search-free</span></div>
  </div>
  <KV label="The master paradox">Programs search millions of nodes; masters explore under 100 and play better. Intelligence lies in extracting information from the problem space to guide search</KV>
</Band>
