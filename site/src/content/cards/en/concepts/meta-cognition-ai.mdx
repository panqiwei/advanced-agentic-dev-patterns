  <p class="card-subtitle">Meta-Cognition — the capacity of a system to monitor, evaluate, and adjust its own reasoning</p>

<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>Cognitive-science definition: cognition about cognition. In AI: a system's ability to detect its own reasoning errors, quantify uncertainty, and adjust strategy accordingly. Three elements: Monitor (detect error signals), Evaluate (quantify uncertainty), Adjust (switch strategy).</CoreDef>
</Band>
