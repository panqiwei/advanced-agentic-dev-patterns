<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>LeCun: "Your mental model of how the world works. You can imagine a sequence of actions you might take; the world model lets you predict the effect of those actions on the world." Core capacities: planning (simulate outcomes before acting), physical reasoning, causal understanding, persistent memory. Complementary to rather than replacement for LLMs: LLMs handle linguistic reasoning and tool use, world models provide physical-environment understanding.</CoreDef>
</Band>
