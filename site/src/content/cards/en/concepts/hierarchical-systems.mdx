<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>Hierarchy = uneven interaction strength: strong coupling within subsystems, weak coupling between them. Social, biological, physical, and symbolic systems all show hierarchical structure — not a coincidence, but the inevitable result of evolutionary pressure.</CoreDef>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-raised" grow>
  <div class="parable">

    <div class="parable-row">
      <div class="pw-col hora">
        <span class="pw-name">Hora ✓</span>
        <span class="pw-desc">Builds stable sub-assemblies first, then assembles; an interruption loses only the last sub-assembly</span>
      </div>
      <div class="pw-col tempus">
        <span class="pw-name">Tempus ✗</span>
        <span class="pw-desc">Assembles in one shot; every interruption means starting over</span>
      </div>
    </div>
    <p class="parable-moral">Corollary: complex systems with stable intermediate forms emerge through evolution far faster than systems without them</p>
  </div>
  <div class="freq-separation">
    <Kicker>Frequency Separation</Kicker>
    <KV label="High-frequency">Dynamics inside subsystems (subsystems are near-independent in the short term)</KV>
    <KV label="Low-frequency">Aggregated between-subsystem interaction emerges as whole-system behavior</KV>
  </div>
  <div class="agentic-mapping">
    <KV label="Harness mapping">system prompt (global / low-freq) → tool definitions (local / mid-freq) → permissions (boundary / high-freq)</KV>
