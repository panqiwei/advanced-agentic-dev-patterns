<Band tone="paper-raised" dense>
  <h2 class="card-title">Orchestrator-Workers</h2>
  <p class="card-subtitle">Orchestrator-Workers — a central LLM decides subtasks on the fly, worker LLMs execute in parallel</p>
</Band>

<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>The orchestrator decides on-the-fly, per input, how to split the task and dispatch to workers. Key difference from parallelization: subtasks are not predefined. Fits cases where subtasks can't be anticipated — e.g. which files to change depends on the task.</CoreDef>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-raised" grow>
  <div class="compare-box">
    <Kicker>Contrast with parallelization</Kicker>
    <div class="cmp-row cmp-header"><span>Dimension</span><span>Parallelization</span><span>Orchestrator-Workers</span></div>
    <div class="cmp-row"><span>Subtasks</span><span>predefined, fixed</span><span>dynamic, input-dependent</span></div>
    <div class="cmp-row"><span>Fits</span><span>batch work that splits cleanly</span><span>complex, unpredictable tasks</span></div>
    <div class="cmp-row"><span>Overhead</span><span>low (no orchestrator calls)</span><span>high (extra orchestrator LLM calls)</span></div>
    <div class="cmp-row"><span>Flexibility</span><span>low</span><span>high</span></div>
  </div>
  <div class="a2a-extend">
    <Kicker>A2A protocol extension</Kicker>
    <KV label="Intra-system">orchestrator LLM + worker LLMs sharing process and tools</KV>
    <KV label="Cross-system (A2A)">orchestrator discovers capabilities via Agent Card, delegates a Task, receives results over SSE</KV>
    <KV label="Typical uses">coding products (which files change depends on the task) / multi-source search and synthesis</KV>
  </div>
</Band>
