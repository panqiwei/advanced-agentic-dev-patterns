  <p class="card-subtitle">Neurosymbolic AI — not either/or, but a principled union of two computational paradigms</p>

<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>Deep learning (distributed representations) is good at perception and learning; symbolic systems (localist) are good at reasoning and knowledge representation. Their complementarity is structural: neural nets handle existential quantification (∃x), symbols handle universal quantification (∀x). The goal is a single system with both capabilities.</CoreDef>
</Band>
