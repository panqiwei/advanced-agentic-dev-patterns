  <p class="card-subtitle">No Free Lunch — no "universal" learning algorithm is uniformly better than any other across all problems</p>
</Band>

<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>Under a uniform prior over all logically possible data sequences, every learning algorithm's expected generalization error = 1/2 (matches random guessing). This is the precise mathematical incarnation of Hume's first horn: no algorithm's generalization can be vindicated a priori. Wolpert 1992/1996.</CoreDef>
</Band>
