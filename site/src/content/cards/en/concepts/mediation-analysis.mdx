<Band tone="paper-raised" dense>
  <h2 class="card-title">Mediation Analysis</h2>
  <p class="card-subtitle">Mediation Analysis — how X reaches Y: X→Y (direct) vs X→M→Y (indirect via mediator)</p>
</Band>

<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>{"When X affects Y both directly and through a mediator M, how do we split the two paths? TE = total effect; NDE = natural direct effect (M held at its counterfactual value); NIE = natural indirect effect; TE = NDE + NIE. NDE can't be expressed with the do-operator — it requires counterfactuals."}</CoreDef>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-raised" grow>
  <div class="effect-grid">
    <Kicker>Four kinds of effect</Kicker>
    <div class="eg-row te"><span class="eg-code">TE</span><span class="eg-name">Total Effect</span><span class="eg-def">{"E[Y|do(X=1)] - E[Y|do(X=0)]"}</span></div>
    <div class="eg-row cde"><span class="eg-code">CDE</span><span class="eg-name">Controlled Direct Effect</span><span class="eg-def">{"do(X=1,M=m) vs do(X=0,M=m); M pinned by fiat"}</span></div>
    <div class="eg-row nde"><span class="eg-code">NDE</span><span class="eg-name">Natural Direct Effect</span><span class="eg-def">{"M takes its X=0 natural value; expressible only via counterfactuals"}</span></div>
    <div class="eg-row nie"><span class="eg-code">NIE</span><span class="eg-name">Natural Indirect Effect</span><span class="eg-def">{"X fixed at 1, M shifts from its X=0 value to its X=1 value"}</span></div>
  </div>
  <div class="formula-note">
    <KV label="Mediation formula">{"P(y|do(x)) = Σ_m P(y|x,m)·P(m|x₀) — key to identifying NDE"}</KV>
    <KV label="Why counterfactuals">{"NDE asks: X actually = 1 while M stays at its hypothetical X=0 value — rung 3 of the ladder"}</KV>
    <KV label="Confounding threat">{"a confounder shared by M and Y blocks NDE identification; extra assumptions required"}</KV>
  </div>
</Band>
