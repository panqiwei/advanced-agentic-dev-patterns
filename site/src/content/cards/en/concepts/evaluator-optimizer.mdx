  <p class="card-subtitle">Evaluator-Optimizer — GAN-style generate-discriminate loop</p>
</Band>

<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>One LLM generates, another evaluates and gives feedback, and the loop iterates. Roles are fixed — not an autonomous agent. Applies when there are clear evaluation criteria and iteration yields measurable gains.</CoreDef>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-raised" grow>
  <div class="loop-diagram">
    <div class="ld-box generator">

    </div>
    <div class="ld-arrow">→</div>
    <div class="ld-box evaluator">
      <span class="ld-label">Evaluator</span>
      <span class="ld-desc">scores + feedback</span>
    </div>
    <div class="ld-arrow loop-back">↰ iterate</div>
  </div>
  <div class="engineering-notes">
    <Kicker>engineering practice (Anthropic)</Kicker>
    <KV label="self-eval bias">an agent judging its own output skews positive; a separate evaluator lets strictness be tuned independently</KV>
    <KV label="operationalize criteria">break subjective axes (originality / craft) into scored items + few-shot calibration examples</KV>
    <KV label="Sprint Contracts">generator and evaluator negotiate "done criteria" before each round, refining spec into testable conditions</KV>
    <KV label="marginal-value convergence">the stronger the model, the more the evaluator's marginal value concentrates at the capability frontier</KV>
  </div>
</Band>
