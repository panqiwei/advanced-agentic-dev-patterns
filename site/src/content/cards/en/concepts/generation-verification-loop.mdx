<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>AI generates, humans verify. Unlike Evaluator-Optimizer (fully automated), the verifier here is human — a fundamental distinction. The speed of this loop caps the efficiency of human-AI collaboration.</CoreDef>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-raised" grow>
  <div class="two-directions">
    <div class="direction fast-verify">
      <Kicker>Direction 1: speed up verification</Kicker>
      <KV label="GUI > text">Red/green diffs are read at a glance; Cmd+Y / Cmd+N is 10× faster than typing</KV>
      <KV label="Use the visual GPU">The visual cortex massively parallelizes; graphical feedback is the "highway"</KV>
    </div>
    <div class="direction constrain-gen">
      <Kicker>Direction 2: constrain generation (keep on leash)</Kicker>
      <KV label="Reject 10K-line diffs">Instant AI output is meaningless — the human is still the verification bottleneck</KV>
      <KV label="Small increments + specific prompts">Vague prompt → failed verification → spin cycle; specific prompt → high pass rate</KV>
      <KV label="Auditable intermediate artifacts">Insert human-auditable intermediates between AI and final output to prevent drift</KV>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="vs-note">
    <span class="vs-label">vs Evaluator-Optimizer</span>
    <span class="vs-text">There the evaluator is also an LLM — fully automated. Here the verifier is human, so AI generation scope must match human verification capacity</span>
  </div>
</Band>
