  <h2 class="card-title">Bag of Heuristics</h2>
  <p class="card-subtitle">Bag of Heuristics — a scattered implementation without a unified algorithm</p>
</Band>

<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>The model didn't implement one unified, composable algorithm — it accumulated many local, position-specific if-then rules whose aggregate effect approximates a "world model" in the macro view, with no central coordinator.</CoreDef>
</Band>
