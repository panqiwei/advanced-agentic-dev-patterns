  <p class="card-subtitle">Reliability Surface — extend single-number pass@1 into a 3D "terrain map"</p>
</Band>

<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>{"pass@1 only measures blood pressure; the reliability surface is a full physical. Three dimensions: k (probability of succeeding k times in a row on the same task), ε (success rate under semantics-preserving perturbations), λ (success rate under infrastructure faults). The flatter and higher the surface, the more production-ready."}</CoreDef>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-raised" grow>
  <div class="three-dims">
    <Kicker>Three dimensions</Kicker>
    <div class="dim-row dk">
      <span class="dim-label">k — consistency</span>
      <span class="dim-def">Probability of succeeding on k repeated runs of the same task (pass^k)</span>
      <span class="dim-insight">k=2 already exposes non-deterministic failure</span>
    </div>
    <div class="dim-row de">
      <span class="dim-label">ε — robustness</span>
      <span class="dim-def">Success rate under semantically equivalent but syntactically different perturbations</span>
      <span class="dim-insight">Prompt rewording should not change outcomes</span>
    </div>
    <div class="dim-row dl">
      <span class="dim-label">λ — fault tolerance</span>
      <span class="dim-def">Success rate under timeouts, rate limits, malformed data, etc.</span>
      <span class="dim-insight">Faults are unavoidable in production</span>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="empirical">
    <Kicker>ReliabilityBench empirics (1280 episodes)</Kicker>
    <div class="emp-row"><span class="emp-cond">Baseline (ε=0, λ=0)</span><span class="emp-val">96.88%</span></div>
    <div class="emp-row"><span class="emp-cond">Perturbation ε=0.2</span><span class="emp-val">88.12% (-8.76pp)</span></div>
    <div class="emp-row"><span class="emp-cond">Faults λ=0.2</span><span class="emp-val">91.0% (-5.88pp)</span></div>
    <div class="emp-row emp-double"><span class="emp-cond">Double stress</span><span class="emp-val">84.0% (-12.88pp ≠ sum of both)</span></div>
  </div>
</Band>
