<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>The blind spot of traditional single-issue-fix benchmarks (SWE-Bench): they cannot predict multi-step evolution ability. SWE-EVO shows GPT-5.2 fixes single steps at 72.8%, but drops to ~20% on multi-step evolution — a gap single-step benchmarks cannot predict. Real software engineering is 80% maintenance and evolution, not single-bug fixes.</CoreDef>
</Band>
