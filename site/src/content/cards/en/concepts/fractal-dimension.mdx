<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>An extension of Euclidean integer dimension: a smooth line is 1-D, a plane is 2-D, a coastline falls between 1 and 2. Cover the object with boxes of side ε; the box count scales as ε⁻ᴰ, and D is the fractal dimension.</CoreDef>
</Band>
