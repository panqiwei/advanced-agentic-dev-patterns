<Band tone="paper-raised" dense>
  <h2 class="card-title">Regularity Theory</h2>
  <p class="card-subtitle">Regularity Theory — causation = constant conjunction between event-types, rejecting causal powers and necessary connections</p>
</Band>

<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>The Humean tradition: causation is not a hidden power but an observable pattern. Three elements: temporal precedence + spatiotemporal contiguity + constant conjunction (every C is followed by an E). Signature stance: reject any metaphysically thick necessary connection. Type-level causation is primary — token-level causation gets its success conditions through type-level regularities.</CoreDef>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-raised" grow>
  <div class="evolution">
    <Kicker>Four-stage evolution of the theory</Kicker>
    <div class="ev-row ev1"><span class="ev-era">Hume original (HRT)</span><span class="ev-key">Three conditions: contiguity + precedence + constant conjunction; two virtues: no heavy metaphysics + explains how causes are discovered</span></div>
    <div class="ev-row ev2"><span class="ev-era">Mill (MRT)</span><span class="ev-key">Refinement: causation needs "totality" conditions + regularities must be genuine laws (best-systems view)</span></div>
    <div class="ev-row ev3"><span class="ev-era">Mackie INUS (1965)</span><span class="ev-key">Cause = INUS condition; relaxes the strict constant-conjunction requirement; introduces causal fields and minimal sufficient clusters</span></div>
    <div class="ev-row ev4"><span class="ev-era">Baumgartner (2013)</span><span class="ev-key">Permanent non-redundancy + active causal paths; handles preemption and overdetermination</span></div>
  </div>
  <div class="challenges">
    <KV label="Open challenges">Token causation (one-off events) · spurious causation (joint effects of a common cause) · causal direction</KV>
    <KV label="Natural successor">Inferential causation: replace regularity-instantiation with inferential relations — keeps the spirit, overcomes the token-causation problem</KV>
  </div>
</Band>
