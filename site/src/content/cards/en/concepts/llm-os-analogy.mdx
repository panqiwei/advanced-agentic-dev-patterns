<Band tone="paper-raised" dense>
  <h2 class="card-title">LLM-OS Analogy</h2>
  <p class="card-subtitle">LLM = CPU, Agent = OS kernel — not a metaphor, a structural isomorphism</p>
</Band>

<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>The LLM is "the kernel process of an emerging operating system" (Karpathy 2023). Not a chatbot, not a word generator. The key asymmetry: traditional OSs sit on deterministic hardware, while agents sit on statistical LLMs — design must account for uncertainty.</CoreDef>
</Band>
