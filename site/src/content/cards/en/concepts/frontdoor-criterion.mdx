<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>Backdoor handles observable confounders; frontdoor handles unobservable confounders when a measurable mediator is available. Two-step estimation: first X→Z (no backdoor), then Z→Y (adjusting for X as a backdoor), finally combine.</CoreDef>
</Band>
