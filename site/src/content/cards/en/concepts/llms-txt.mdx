<Band tone="paper-raised" dense>
  <h2 class="card-title">llms.txt</h2>
  <p class="card-subtitle">robots.txt tells crawlers what they may crawl; llms.txt tells LLMs what this site is</p>
</Band>

<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>A Markdown file placed at site /llms.txt — used primarily at inference time (when a user queries the LLM), not at training time. The site proactively supplies an LLM-parsable format instead of waiting for the LLM to crawl and parse HTML itself.</CoreDef>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-raised" grow>
  <div class="format-spec">
    <Kicker>Format spec</Kicker>
    <div class="fs-row"><span class="fs-req">REQ</span><span class="fs-el">H1</span><span class="fs-desc">Project or site name</span></div>
    <div class="fs-row"><span class="fs-opt">OPT</span><span class="fs-el">Blockquote</span><span class="fs-desc">One-sentence summary (required for understanding the rest)</span></div>
    <div class="fs-row"><span class="fs-opt">OPT</span><span class="fs-el">Markdown paragraphs</span><span class="fs-desc">Additional background, no headings</span></div>
    <div class="fs-row"><span class="fs-opt">OPT</span><span class="fs-el">H2 sections</span><span class="fs-desc">Annotated hyperlink lists</span></div>
    <div class="fs-row special"><span class="fs-spec">SPC</span><span class="fs-el">Optional section</span><span class="fs-desc">Skippable when context is tight — secondary resources</span></div>
  </div>
