<Band tone="paper-raised" dense>
  <h2 class="card-title">Necessary Connection</h2>
  <p class="card-subtitle">Necessary Connection — given the cause, the effect must follow — but where does this "must" come from?</p>
</Band>

<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>Traditional philosophy treated causation as an objective necessary tie out in the world. Hume's critique is radical: all external observation delivers is constant conjunction, never any "connection" itself. Once the three candidate sources are ruled out, the necessary connection has to be relocated — to the human mind.</CoreDef>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-raised" grow>
  <div class="elimination">
    <Kicker>Hume's three-step elimination</Kicker>
    <div class="elim-row ex1">
      <span class="e-src">External impressions (Locke)</span>
      <span class="e-verdict">✗ out</span>
      <span class="e-reason">colliding billiard balls: we see succession, never the "connection" itself</span>
    </div>
    <div class="elim-row ex2">
      <span class="e-src">Internal impressions (will)</span>
      <span class="e-verdict">✗ out</span>
      <span class="e-reason">moving a finger: intention precedes motion, but the mechanism is wholly opaque</span>
    </div>
    <div class="elim-row ex3">
      <span class="e-src">Divine will (Malebranche)</span>
      <span class="e-verdict">✗ out</span>
      <span class="e-reason">beyond any possible experience — it drags us into "fairyland"</span>
    </div>
    <div class="elim-row conclusion">
      <span class="e-src">Mind's felt determination</span>
      <span class="e-verdict">✓ source</span>
      <span class="e-reason">the inward feeling of being led from one idea to another by habit</span>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="aftermath">
    <KV label="Kant's reply">synthetic a priori: causality is a pure category of the understanding that makes experience possible — neither from experience nor from habit</KV>
    <KV label="Regularity theory">faithful to Hume: causation = constant conjunction; rejects any metaphysically thick "necessary connection"</KV>
  </div>
</Band>
