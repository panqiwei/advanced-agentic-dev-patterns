<Band tone="paper-raised" dense>
  <h2 class="card-title">Ladder of Causation</h2>
  <p class="card-subtitle">Ladder of Causation — from seeing to doing to imagining; each rung answers what lower rungs cannot</p>
</Band>

<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>Three irreducible rungs: Rung 1 (association, P(Y|X)) cannot answer Rung 2 (intervention, P(Y|do(X))); Rung 2 cannot answer Rung 3 (counterfactual). More data or bigger models alone cannot jump from association to intervention — causal structure knowledge is required.</CoreDef>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-raised" grow>
  <div class="three-rungs">
    <div class="rung rung-1">
      <div class="rung-left">
        <span class="rung-num">Ⅰ</span>
        <span class="rung-name">Association</span>
      </div>
      <div class="rung-right">
        <span class="rung-math">P(Y | X)</span>
        <span class="rung-verb">Seeing · observing</span>
        <span class="rung-q">"Given I observe X, what is P(Y)?"</span>
        <span class="rung-limit limit-bad">Most ML lives here. Ice cream ≠ drownings</span>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="rung rung-2">
      <div class="rung-left">
        <span class="rung-num">Ⅱ</span>
        <span class="rung-name">Intervention</span>
      </div>
      <div class="rung-right">
        <span class="rung-math">P(Y | do(X))</span>
        <span class="rung-verb">Doing · acting</span>
        <span class="rung-q">"If I do X, what happens to Y?"</span>
        <span class="rung-limit">RCTs / causal DAG + do-calculus</span>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="rung rung-3">
      <div class="rung-left">
        <span class="rung-num">Ⅲ</span>
        <span class="rung-name">Counterfactual</span>
      </div>
      <div class="rung-right">
        <span class="rung-math">{"P(Y_x(u))"}</span>
        <span class="rung-verb">Imagining</span>
        <span class="rung-q">"Had X been different, would Y have been different?"</span>
        <span class="rung-limit">Requires a full SCM; abduction → action → prediction</span>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="ns-note">
    <span class="ns-label">Neurosymbolic angle</span>
    <span class="ns-text">Pure deep learning lives on Rung 1 (association). Neurosymbolic systems can climb all three: once a network encodes symbolic descriptions (A→B), intervention and counterfactual queries become possible</span>
  </div>
</Band>
