  <h2 class="card-title">Action Metamorphic Relations</h2>
  <p class="card-subtitle">Formal measurement of agent robustness — equality anchored at the terminal state</p>
</Band>

<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>Apply semantic-equivalent transforms to the task description. The equality anchor is not the output text but the **world's terminal state**. "Book a flight from New York to Los Angeles" and "I need to go from NYC to LAX" — steps may differ, terminal states must match.</CoreDef>
</Band>
