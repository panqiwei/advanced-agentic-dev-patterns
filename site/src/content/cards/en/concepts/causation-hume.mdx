<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>Critique: a priori reasoning fails; empirical reasoning is circular (presupposes uniformity); necessary connection is unobservable — only constant conjunction is. Construction: custom/habit, not reason, is the real engine of causal inference.</CoreDef>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-raised" grow>
  <div class="hume-structure">
    <div class="hs-col hs-critique">
      <Kicker>Critical phase (reason's foundation dissolves)</Kicker>
      <ul class="hs-list">
        <li>A priori: one who has never seen fire cannot infer fire burns</li>
        <li>Empirical: relies on future resembling past → needs uniformity → circular</li>
        <li>Necessary connection: experience shows only <em>conjunction</em>, never <em>connection</em></li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="hs-col hs-construct">
      <Kicker>Constructive phase (habit is the real basis)</Kicker>
      <ul class="hs-list">
        <li>After constant conjunction, habit forms an "expectation" disposition</li>
        <li>The idea of necessary connection comes from the "determination of the mind" — an inner impression</li>
        <li>Necessity lies in us, not in the things</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
</Band>
