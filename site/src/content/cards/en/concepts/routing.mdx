<Band tone="paper-raised" dense>
  <h2 class="card-title">Routing</h2>
  <p class="card-subtitle">Routing — classify the input, then dispatch it to a specialized downstream pipeline</p>
</Band>

<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>Routing achieves separation of concerns: different input types are handled by different dedicated prompts or models, so optimizing for one type does not hurt the others. Classification can be done by an LLM or a traditional classifier. Fits when tasks have clear categories and classification is reliable.</CoreDef>
</Band>
