  <p class="card-subtitle">Reliability Decay — agent success rate drops super-linearly as task complexity grows</p>

<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>Not the trivial observation that "harder tasks fail more often." Decay is super-linear — faster than an independent-errors assumption predicts. Root cause: errors are positively correlated across steps (ρ &gt; 0) — once an agent takes a wrong turn, it tends to stay wrong and does not spontaneously self-correct.</CoreDef>
</Band>
