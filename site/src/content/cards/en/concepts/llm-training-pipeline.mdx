<Band tone="paper-raised" dense>
  <h2 class="card-title">LLM Training Pipeline</h2>
  <p class="card-subtitle">Raw internet text → usable conversational assistant via multi-stage transformation</p>
</Band>

<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>Pre-training is not naive information storage — the prediction task forces the model to absorb world knowledge. Fine-tuning changes format, not knowledge. The core contrast: quantity (massive, low-quality) vs quality (small, high-quality); training load: pre-train 10TB + 12 days vs fine-tune 100K conversations + 1 day.</CoreDef>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-raised" grow>
  <div class="three-stages">
    <div class="stage stage1">
      <span class="stage-num">01</span>
      <div class="stage-content">
        <span class="stage-name">Pre-training</span>
        <div class="stage-details">
          <span>Input: ~10TB internet text</span>
          <span>Objective: next-token prediction (NTP)</span>
          <span>Compute: ~6000 GPUs × 12 days</span>
          <span>Output: base model (~100× lossy compression)</span>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="stage stage2">
      <span class="stage-num">02</span>
      <div class="stage-content">
        <span class="stage-name">Fine-tuning</span>
        <div class="stage-details">
          <span>Input: ~100K human-curated Q&A</span>
          <span>Objective: still next-token prediction, but on high-quality dialog data</span>
          <span>Output: assistant model (document generator → question answerer)</span>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="stage stage3">
      <span class="stage-num">03</span>
      <div class="stage-content">
        <span class="stage-name">RLHF (optional)</span>
        <div class="stage-details">
          <span>Input: human preference labels (A better than B)</span>
          <span>Advantage: judging which is better is easier than writing a good answer from scratch</span>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
  <KV label="Evolving trend">Human labeling is being replaced by human-AI collaboration: models sample candidates → humans select → training data. As models strengthen, the human role shifts from "author" to "reviewer"</KV>
</Band>
