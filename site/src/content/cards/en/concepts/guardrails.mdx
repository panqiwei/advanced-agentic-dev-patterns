<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>Continuously monitor agent inputs, outputs, and actions at runtime to keep them in bounds. Harness imposes architectural constraints at compile time; guardrails act at runtime. Optimistic execution: the agent proceeds normally, guardrails check concurrently, and violations trigger an interrupt.</CoreDef>
</Band>
