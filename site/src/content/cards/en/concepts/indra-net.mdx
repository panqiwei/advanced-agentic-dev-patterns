<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>An infinite net of jewels hanging in Indra's palace; each jewel reflects every other jewel (recursion without end). A local change is a global change — alter one, and all reflections update at once. Predates Mandelbrot's fractal concept by roughly 1,300 years.</CoreDef>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-raised" grow>
  <Quote cite="Francis H. Cook, 1973">In each mesh of the net hangs a jewel, and the jewels are infinite in number. Look closely at any one and you will find that its surface reflects every other jewel in the net — and each reflected jewel is itself reflecting all the others, producing an endless process of reflection... an infinite web of mutual relation among all members.</Quote>
