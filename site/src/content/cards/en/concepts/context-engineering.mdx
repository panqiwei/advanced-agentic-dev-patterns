<Band tone="paper-raised" dense>
  <h2 class="card-title">Context Engineering</h2>
  <p class="card-subtitle">Context Engineering — the umbrella term for curating and maintaining the optimal token set</p>
</Band>

<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>Find the smallest high-signal token set that maximizes the likelihood of the desired outcome. Prompt engineering writes good instructions (static); context engineering curates information (dynamic) — every inference is a fresh decision about what to include and what to drop.</CoreDef>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-raised" grow>
  <PillarGrid cols={2}>
    <Pillar label="attention budget">Transformer's n² pairwise relations — as tokens grow, attention dilutes; every token spends the budget</Pillar>
    <Pillar label="just-in-time">keep lightweight references (paths / queries / links) and load them dynamically at runtime — beats upfront stuffing</Pillar>
    <Pillar label="compaction">when the window is exhausted, compress history; structured summaries (explicit sections) beat full regeneration</Pillar>
    <Pillar label="sub-agent">split long tasks into multiple isolated contexts; externalize cross-session state (progress file)</Pillar>
  </PillarGrid>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-sunk" dense>
