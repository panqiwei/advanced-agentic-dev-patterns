<Band tone="paper-raised" dense>
  <h2 class="card-title">Othello World Model Hypothesis</h2>
  <p class="card-subtitle">OWMH — a model trained only on move sequences spontaneously develops an internal board representation</p>
</Band>

<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>OthelloGPT experiment: an 8-layer GPT trained only on move sequences, with no board geometry as input. Internal activations contain a structured representation of the board state. A small, controlled case of connectionist networks emerging symbolic structure. Li et al. 2022 → Nanda et al. 2023 → Yuan & Søgaard 2025.</CoreDef>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-raised" grow>
  <div class="three-gen">
    <Kicker>Three generations of evidence</Kicker>
    <div class="gen-row g1">
      <span class="gen-auth">Li et al. 2022</span>
      <span class="gen-find">nonlinear probe drops error rate to 1.7%; causal intervention shows the representation affects output</span>
    </div>
    <div class="gen-row g2">
      <span class="gen-auth">Nanda et al. 2023</span>
      <span class="gen-find">correct the frame (relative color) → linear probe accuracy jumps 75% → 99%; the representation is linear</span>
    </div>
    <div class="gen-row g3">
      <span class="gen-auth">Yuan & Søgaard 2025</span>
      <span class="gen-find">across 7 LLMs; unsupervised Procrustes alignment hits 99%; cross-architecture cosine similarity 93–96%</span>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="key-constraints">
    <Kicker>Key constraints and implications</Kicker>
    <KV label="Repr. ≠ planning">2-hop prediction degrades sharply — high-accuracy state representation doesn't automatically yield multi-step planning</KV>
    <KV label="Bag of heuristics">MATS analysis: the mechanism is an aggregation of local heuristics, not a unified board-tracking algorithm</KV>
    <KV label="Cross-arch convergence">different architectures converge on similar representations — hints at representation attractors independent of the network</KV>
  </div>
</Band>
