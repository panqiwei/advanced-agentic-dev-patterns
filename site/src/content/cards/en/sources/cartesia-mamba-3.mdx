  <p class="card-subtitle">Mamba-2 was designed for training efficiency · Mamba-3 is explicitly inference-first — a response to the surge of agentic workloads</p>

<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>All three improvements are inspired by <span class="bk">classical control theory and the state-space model literature</span> — cutting against the current linear-attention / test-time-training fashion. Core insight: linear models do too little compute per decode step; GPUs spend most of their time shuffling memory — so <span class="bk">adding FLOPs per step has almost no impact on inference latency</span>.</CoreDef>
</Band>
