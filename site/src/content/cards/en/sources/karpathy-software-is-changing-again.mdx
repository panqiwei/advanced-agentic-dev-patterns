<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>LLMs wear three hats at once — <span class="t">utility</span> (metered access · downtime = "intellectual blackout") + <span class="t">fab</span> (high capex · deep tech tree) + <span class="t">OS</span> (closed vs open = Windows/macOS vs Linux). "We're in the 1960s of LLMs" — centralized compute, time-sharing, the GUI still uninvented.</CoreDef>
</Band>
