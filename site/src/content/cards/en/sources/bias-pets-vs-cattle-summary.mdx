      "Is this server an irreplaceable individual, or a replaceable member of a herd?"

  <div class="bridge">
    <div class="br-row">

      <span class="br-arrow">→</span>
      <span class="br-to">Brain / Hands / Session decoupled</span>
    </div>
    <div class="br-row">
      <span class="br-from">container failure = lost session</span>
      <span class="br-arrow">→</span>
      <span class="br-to">session externalized · wake() recovers</span>
    </div>
    <div class="br-row">
      <span class="br-from">credentials in container</span>
      <span class="br-arrow">→</span>
      <span class="br-to">credentials never enter the sandbox</span>
    </div>
