<Band tone="paper-raised" dense>
  <h2 class="card-title">The Coastline Paradox</h2>
  <p class="card-subtitle">A coastline has no determinate length — the finer the measurement, the longer it becomes</p>
</Band>

<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>Ordinary smooth curves are <span class="em">rectifiable</span> — their total length converges. Coastlines are not: every scale reveals new detail, and no convergence limit exists. This gives a coastline a fractal dimension <span class="hl">1 &lt; D &lt; 2</span>.</CoreDef>
</Band>
