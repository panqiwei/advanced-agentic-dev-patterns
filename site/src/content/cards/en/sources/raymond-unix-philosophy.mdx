  <h2 class="card-title">The Elements of Operating-System Style</h2>
  <p class="card-subtitle">How Unix's unifying ideas shape development style — the opposite design is the "perfect anti-Unix"</p>
</Band>

<Band tone="tan" dense>
  <CoreDef>
    <span class="q">"In Unix, one tries to design programs to operate not specifically with each other, but with <span class="em">programs as yet unthought of</span>."</span> — Ken Thompson
  </CoreDef>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-raised" grow>
  <div class="dim-head">Seven dimensions · Unix style vs anti-Unix</div>
  <div class="dims">
    <div class="dm">
      <div class="dm-k">Unifying idea</div>
      <div class="dm-u">"everything is a file" + pipes</div>
      <div class="dm-a">no unifying idea · ad-hoc feature pileup</div>
    </div>
    <div class="dm">
      <div class="dm-k">Multitasking</div>
      <div class="dm-u">preemptive</div>
      <div class="dm-a">not supported · or crippled to near-unusable</div>
    </div>
    <div class="dm">
      <div class="dm-k">Cooperating processes</div>
      <div class="dm-u">cheap spawn + flexible IPC</div>
      <div class="dm-a">expensive spawn · half-baked IPC</div>
    </div>
    <div class="dm">
      <div class="dm-k">Internal boundaries</div>
      <div class="dm-u">MMU + user permissions + minimal trusted code</div>
      <div class="dm-a">weak permission groups · large trusted base</div>
    </div>
    <div class="dm">
      <div class="dm-k">File attributes</div>
      <div class="dm-u">no record structure · byte streams</div>
      <div class="dm-a">heavy record structure · reliance on attributes</div>
    </div>
    <div class="dm">
      <div class="dm-k">File formats</div>
      <div class="dm-u">text transparency · filter ecosystem</div>
      <div class="dm-a">binary everywhere · needs heavy tools to read/write</div>
    </div>
    <div class="dm">
      <div class="dm-k">Preferred interface</div>
      <div class="dm-u">CLI + GUI equally · scriptable</div>
      <div class="dm-a">no CLI · critical features non-scriptable</div>
    </div>
  </div>
</Band>

  </div>
