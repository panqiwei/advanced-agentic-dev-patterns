  <p class="card-subtitle">Shared structural properties of complex systems — in the architecture, not the content</p>

<Band tone="paper-raised" grow>
  <div class="four-parts">
    <div class="pt pt--1">
      <div class="pt-n">Ⅰ</div>
      <div class="pt-k">Hierarchic systems</div>
      <div class="pt-v">Subsystems nested in subsystems — social / biological / physical / symbolic systems all exhibit hierarchy. Interaction strength unifies spatial proximity and social organization</div>
    </div>
    <div class="pt pt--2">
      <div class="pt-n">Ⅱ</div>
      <div class="pt-k">Evolution of complex systems</div>
      <div class="pt-v"><span class="em">Watchmaker parable</span> — systems with stable intermediate forms evolve far faster than those without</div>
    </div>
    <div class="pt pt--3">
      <div class="pt-n">Ⅲ</div>
      <div class="pt-k">Near-decomposability</div>
      <div class="pt-v">Within-subsystem interaction ≫ between-subsystem — in the short run subsystems are approximately independent; in the long run they interact only in aggregate</div>
    </div>
    <div class="pt pt--4">
      <div class="pt-n">Ⅳ</div>
      <div class="pt-k">Description of complexity</div>
      <div class="pt-v">Outline form is its natural language — <span class="em">the description of a complex system need not be as large as the system itself</span>; description complexity depends on the representation, not the object</div>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="parable">
    <Kicker>Watchmaker parable</Kicker>
    <div class="pb-grid">
      <div class="pb-col pb-col--h">
        <div class="pb-name">Hora</div>
        <div class="pb-strategy">Assembles sub-assemblies of 10 · layer by layer</div>
        <div class="pb-result pb-result--good">✓ Interruption loses only one group</div>
      </div>
      <div class="pb-col pb-col--t">
        <div class="pb-name">Tempus</div>
        <div class="pb-strategy">Assembles all parts in one go</div>
        <div class="pb-result pb-result--bad">✗ Interrupted = start over · goes bankrupt</div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</Band>

<Band tone="tan" dense>
  <CoreDef>
    <span class="em">The role of partial progress in problem solving</span> is the same as that of stable subassemblies in evolution. Two sources of selectivity: iterative trial-and-error + previous experience (reduction to already-solved problems).
  </CoreDef>
</Band>
