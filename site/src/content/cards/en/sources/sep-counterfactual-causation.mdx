  <h2 class="card-title">Counterfactual Theories of Causation</h2>
  <p class="card-subtitle">c causes e ⇔ had c not occurred, e would not have occurred — a forty-year research programme</p>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-raised" grow>
  <div class="evo">
    <div class="ev ev--1">
      <div class="ev-year">1973</div>
      <div class="ev-title">Lewis's founding</div>
      <div class="ev-points">
        <div class="ep">· causal dependence: e depends on c ⇔ "if ¬c, then ¬e"</div>
        <div class="ep">· causal chain: transitive closure of dependence</div>
        <div class="ep">· possible-worlds semantics + temporal asymmetry</div>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="ev ev--2">
      <div class="ev-year">2000</div>
      <div class="ev-title">Lewis's revision</div>
      <div class="ev-points">
        <div class="ep">· shift from "dependence" to <span class="em">"influence"</span></div>
        <div class="ep">· changes to c (in time/manner) → corresponding changes to e</div>
        <div class="ep">· "alterations" of events</div>
        <div class="ep-warn">⚠ spurious causation + problem of a metric</div>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="ev ev--3">
      <div class="ev-year">2001-</div>
      <div class="ev-title">Structural equations framework (SEF)</div>
      <div class="ev-points">
        <div class="ep">· Hitchcock · Halpern &amp; Pearl · Woodward</div>
        <div class="ep">· <span class="mono">⟨V, E⟩</span> surgical intervention · active causal route</div>
        <div class="ep">· freeze off-path variables → test dependence</div>
        <div class="ep-new">✓ handles late &amp; trumping preemption</div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</Band>

<Band tone="tan" dense>
  <CoreDef>Late preemption (Billy-Suzy throwing rocks) and trumping preemption are the <span class="em">most persistent challenges</span> for counterfactual theories. SEF supplies a more powerful toolkit than Lewis's original theory via its "freeze variables + active route" technique.</CoreDef>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-sunk" dense>

    <span class="cv">Invariantism (Lewis) / contrastivism (Schaffer: "c rather than c* causes e rather than e*") / contextualism — still unresolved</span>
  </div>
</Band>
