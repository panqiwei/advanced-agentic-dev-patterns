<Band tone="tan" grow>
  <CoreDef>
    70 years of AI research keep confirming: <span class="em">methods that leverage general computation ultimately win by a large margin</span>. Researchers repeatedly try to bake human domain knowledge into the system — effective and satisfying short-term, stagnating or actively obstructing long-term. Breakthroughs come from the opposite approach: scaling search and learning.
  </CoreDef>
</Band>
