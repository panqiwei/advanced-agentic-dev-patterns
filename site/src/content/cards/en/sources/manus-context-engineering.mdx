    </div>
    <div class="in in--3">
      <div class="in-n">3</div>
      <div class="in-k">Filesystem as infinite memory</div>
      <div class="in-v">URLs / paths must be preserved — <span class="em">unrecoverable compression = data destruction</span></div>
    </div>
    <div class="in in--4">
      <div class="in-n">4</div>
      <div class="in-k"><span class="mono">todo.md</span> as attention manipulation</div>

    </div>
    <div class="in in--5">
      <div class="in-n">5</div>
      <div class="in-k">Keep errors on purpose</div>
      <div class="in-v">Stack traces and error messages = free belief update · erasing them erases recovery capability</div>
    </div>
    <div class="in in--6">
      <div class="in-n">6</div>

      <div class="in-v">Inject structured variation into serialization templates and phrasing — break single-pattern reinforcement</div>
    </div>
  </div>
