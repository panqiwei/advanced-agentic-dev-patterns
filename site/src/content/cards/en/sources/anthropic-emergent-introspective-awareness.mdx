<Band tone="paper-raised" dense>
  <h2 class="card-title">Emergent Introspective Awareness in LLMs</h2>
  <p class="card-subtitle">Using "concept injection" to probe a model's limited-but-real awareness of its own internal states</p>
</Band>

<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>Core method — inject known-concept activation patterns into the model's internals, then observe whether it can detect, identify, and report the injected state. Opus 4/4.1 hit ~20% at the optimal layer and strength — failure remains the norm, but enough to show that introspective capability emerges alongside raw capability.</CoreDef>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-raised">
  <div class="exp">
    <span class="exp__n">01</span>
    <div class="exp__body">
      <div class="exp__title">Injected-thought detection <span class="exp__rate">~20%</span></div>
      <div class="exp__desc">Told that "an artificial thought may have been injected," can the model identify it? Opus 4.1 hits ~20% at the optimal layer and strength</div>
    </div>
  </div>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-sunk">
  <div class="exp">
    <span class="exp__n">02</span>
    <div class="exp__body">
      <div class="exp__title">Thought vs. text distinction <span class="exp__rate">significant</span></div>
      <div class="exp__desc">Can the model simultaneously transcribe the input and report the injected "thought"? All models score well above chance — internal representation of "this is not input" exists</div>
    </div>
  </div>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-raised" grow>
  <div class="exp">
    <span class="exp__n">03</span>
    <div class="exp__body">
      <div class="exp__title">Non-intentional output detection <span class="exp__rate">controllable</span></div>
      <div class="exp__desc">After an unnatural prefill, the model inspects its own activations and concludes "I didn't say this" — but inject the matching concept vector and it accepts the prefill as self-produced</div>
    </div>
  </div>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-sunk">
  <div class="exp">
    <span class="exp__n">04</span>
    <div class="exp__body">
      <div class="exp__title">Internal-state control <span class="exp__rate">across the board</span></div>
      <div class="exp__desc">Instruct the model to "think of a word" while writing a sentence — across all models, activation of that word strengthens on "think" and weakens on "don't think." Active modulation of internal state</div>
    </div>
  </div>
</Band>
