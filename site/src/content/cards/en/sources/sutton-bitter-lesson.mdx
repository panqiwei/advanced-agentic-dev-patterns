<Band tone="paper-raised" dense>
  <h2 class="card-title">The Bitter Lesson</h2>
  <p class="card-subtitle">Search and learning — the two methods that scale without bound. Human domain knowledge wins short term, loses to compute long term.</p>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-raised">
  <Kicker>A pattern that keeps repeating</Kicker>
  <div class="hist">
    <div class="hs"><span class="hs-year">1997</span><span class="hs-domain">Chess</span><span class="hs-v">Deep search beats Kasparov — the knowledge camp "was not a good loser"</span></div>
    <div class="hs"><span class="hs-year">2016</span><span class="hs-domain">Go</span><span class="hs-v">AlphaGo self-play learning — same script, 20 years late</span></div>
    <div class="hs"><span class="hs-year">1970s</span><span class="hs-domain">Speech recognition</span><span class="hs-v">HMM statistics beat hand-crafted phoneme features</span></div>
    <div class="hs"><span class="hs-year">2012</span><span class="hs-domain">Computer vision</span><span class="hs-v">CNN convolutions beat SIFT / edge detection</span></div>
  </div>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-sunk" dense>
  <div class="pattern">
    <div class="pt-head">The four-act pattern</div>
    <div class="pt"><span class="pn">①</span><span class="pv">Researchers try to build knowledge into the agent</span></div>
    <div class="pt"><span class="pn">②</span><span class="pv">It works short term — and it feels satisfying</span></div>
    <div class="pt"><span class="pn">③</span><span class="pv">Long term it plateaus, even blocks progress</span></div>
    <div class="pt"><span class="pn">④</span><span class="pv">Breakthrough arrives from the <span class="em">opposite direction</span> — scale compute · search + learning</span></div>
  </div>
</Band>

<Band tone="tan" dense>
  <CoreDef>
    <span class="q">"We want AI agents that can <span class="em">discover like we can</span>, not which contain what we have discovered."</span> — don't build in the discoveries, build in the discovering.
  </CoreDef>
</Band>
