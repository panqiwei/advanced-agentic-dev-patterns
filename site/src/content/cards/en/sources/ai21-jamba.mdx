<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>Jamba mixes SSM layers (Mamba) with Transformer attention layers and MoE layers, optimizing throughput, memory efficiency, and quality at once. Key ratio: 1-in-8 Transformer attention layers, 7-in-8 Mamba — matching the theoretically optimal ratio. A milestone marking SSM hybrid architectures' move from research to production.</CoreDef>
</Band>
