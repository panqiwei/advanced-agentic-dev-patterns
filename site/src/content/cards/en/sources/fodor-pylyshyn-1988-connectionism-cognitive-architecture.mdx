<Band tone="tan" dense>
  <CoreDef>
    <span class="em">Two-level distinction</span> — connectionism cannot replace classical symbolic architecture at the <span class="mono">cognitive-architecture level</span> (the target of this critique); but it can serve at the <span class="mono">implementation level</span>, studying how classical computation is realized in the neural substrate.
  </CoreDef>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-raised" grow>
  <div class="arg">
    <div class="arg-head">Systematicity argument · the core</div>
    <div class="arg-claim">
      If you can think <span class="tk">"John loves Mary"</span>, you must also be able to think <span class="tk">"Mary loves John"</span> — this connection is <span class="em">not accidental</span> but necessary.
    </div>
    <div class="arg-lanes">
      <div class="lane lane--cls">
        <div class="ln-head">
          <span class="ln-tag ln-tag--cls">Classical</span>
          <span class="ln-name">LOT + compositionality</span>
        </div>
        <div class="ln-body">The mental representation of "John loves Mary" <span class="em">contains</span> JOHN, LOVES, MARY as constituents — automatically supplying all the parts needed to form "Mary loves John"</div>
        <div class="ln-verdict ln-verdict--good">✓ Explains systematicity</div>
      </div>
      <div class="lane lane--con">
        <div class="ln-head">
          <span class="ln-tag ln-tag--con">Connectionist</span>
          <span class="ln-name">Distributed vectors</span>
        </div>
        <div class="ln-body">The two sentences may share no units, no activation pattern, no structural element — whether structurally related sentences are processed depends entirely on training data</div>
        <div class="ln-verdict ln-verdict--bad">✗ Brute contingency</div>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="arg-concl">
      If systematicity is a non-accidental feature of cognition, any architecture that makes it accidental is the <span class="em">wrong architecture</span>
    </div>
  </div>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-sunk" dense>
  <div class="echo">
    <span class="ek">Contemporary echo</span>
    <span class="ev">Constrained decoding, the RANLP 2025 "hidden costs" paper, trajectory bias — the friction of bolting symbolic rules onto neural systems is the systematicity argument in contemporary empirical form</span>
  </div>
</Band>
