<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>In the AAAI 2020 Robert S. Engelmore Memorial Lecture ("The Third AI Summer"), Kautz proposed a six-category architectural taxonomy of neurosymbolic AI that has since become a standard reference frame for the field. His headline thesis: "there will be no third winter" — AI will evolve through neurosymbolic integration rather than end in funding retreat. Against the "cat-and-mouse" narrative: AlphaGo is itself already a Type-2 neurosymbolic system.</CoreDef>
</Band>
