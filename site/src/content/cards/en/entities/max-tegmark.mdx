<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>Tegmark is a well-known cosmologist, best known for the Mathematical Universe Hypothesis and Our Mathematical Universe (2014). He is also an active voice in AI safety, co-founder of the Future of Life Institute (FLI), and an advocate of the 2017 Asilomar AI Principles. In AI interpretability he imports the quantitative methods of physics to the study of LLM internals, with a focus on discovering structured, measurable regularities inside neural networks.</CoreDef>
