<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>Garcez co-authored "Neurosymbolic AI: The 3rd Wave" (arXiv:2012.05876) with Luís C. Lamb, synthesizing 20 years of neurosymbolic computing research, published in Artificial Intelligence Review (Springer Nature, 2023). Core contributions: the systematic argument for propositional fixation, the detailed exposition of the six-class Kautz taxonomy, and the critical reassertion of the XAI fidelity principle.</CoreDef>
</Band>
