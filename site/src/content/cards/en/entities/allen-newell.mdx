<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>Newell's partnership with Herbert Simon is one of the longest-running collaborations in the history of AI (1954–1992). They jointly received the 1975 ACM Turing Award for foundational contributions to AI, the symbolic system theory of cognition, the invention of list processing, and for conceiving of the computer as a system that manipulates symbol structures.</CoreDef>
