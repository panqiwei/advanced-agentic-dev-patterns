<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>The core philosophy of the Avatamsaka Sutra is dharmadhatu arising (法界缘起) — all phenomena are mutually causal, mutually containing, and perfectly interfusing without obstruction. Two metaphors of enduring influence emerged: Sumeru in a mustard seed (大小互含 — the part mapping the whole) and Indra's Net (an endless net of mutually reflecting jewels — each node containing global information). Together they express the core thesis of Huayan philosophy: one is all, all is one.</CoreDef>
</Band>
