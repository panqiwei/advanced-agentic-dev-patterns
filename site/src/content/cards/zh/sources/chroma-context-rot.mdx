<Band tone="tan" dense>
  <CoreDef>方法论贡献——<span class="hl">保持任务难度不变、仅变化输入长度</span>，隔离输入长度本身作为性能退化变量。所有 18 个前沿模型无一例外：更多 token = 性能退化。</CoreDef>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-raised" grow>
  <div class="rot-head">三种退化机制</div>
  <div class="mech">
    <div class="mch mch--1">
      <div class="mch-n">Ⅰ</div>
      <div class="mch-body">
        <div class="mch-k">Needle-Question Similarity</div>
        <div class="mch-v">低相似度配对在短 context 下可成功、在长 context 下退化——退化不源于任务难度，而源于输入长度</div>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="mch mch--2">
      <div class="mch-n">Ⅱ</div>
      <div class="mch-body">
        <div class="mch-k">Distractor Interference</div>
        <div class="mch-v">单个 distractor 即降低性能，四个更糟——<span class="em">不同 distractor 影响非均匀</span>，随输入长度放大</div>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="mch mch--3">
      <div class="mch-n">Ⅲ</div>
      <div class="mch-body">
        <div class="mch-k">Haystack Structure Effect</div>
        <div class="mch-v">反直觉——<span class="em">连贯结构的 haystack 反而比随机打乱更损害性能</span>，注意力机制对输入结构敏感</div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="behavior">
    <Kicker>模型行为差异 · 同压力下的策略分野</Kicker>
    <div class="bv-row">
      <span class="bv-k">Claude</span>
      <span class="bv-v">保守——不确定倾向弃权（"找不到答案"），幻觉率最低</span>
    </div>
    <div class="bv-row">
      <span class="bv-k">GPT</span>
      <span class="bv-v">自信——面对 distractor 倾向生成似是而非的错误答案，幻觉率最高</span>
    </div>
  </div>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-sunk" dense>
  <div class="takeaway">
    <span class="tk-k">关键启示</span>
    <span class="tk-v">NIAH 基准仅测词汇级检索——高分不代表长 context 能力可靠。<span class="em">信息如何呈现</span>与信息是否在 context 中同等重要。</span>
  </div>
</Band>
