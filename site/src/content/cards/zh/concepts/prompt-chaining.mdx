<Band tone="paper-raised" dense>
  <h2 class="card-title">提示链</h2>
  <p class="card-subtitle">Prompt Chaining — 将任务分解为顺序步骤，每步 LLM 调用处理上一步输出</p>
</Band>

<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>Agentic 系统中最简单的 Workflow 模式。任务能干净地分解为固定顺序子任务时使用。本质是用延迟换准确率——让每次 LLM 调用面对更简单、更专注的子任务。可在中间步骤加入程序化 Gate 检查。</CoreDef>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-raised" grow>
  <div class="chain-example">
    <Kicker>典型用例</Kicker>
    <div class="ce-row">
      <div class="ce-step s1"><span class="ce-n">①</span><span class="ce-task">生成营销文案（英文）</span></div>
      <div class="ce-arrow">→</div>
      <div class="ce-step s2"><span class="ce-n">②</span><span class="ce-task">翻译为目标语言</span></div>
    </div>
    <div class="ce-row">
      <div class="ce-step s1"><span class="ce-n">①</span><span class="ce-task">写文档大纲</span></div>
      <div class="ce-arrow">→</div>
      <div class="ce-gate">Gate 检查</div>
      <div class="ce-arrow">→</div>
      <div class="ce-step s2"><span class="ce-n">③</span><span class="ce-task">基于大纲写文档</span></div>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="positioning">
    <Kicker>在 Agentic Workflow 中的定位</Kicker>
    <KV label="适用场景">子任务固定且有序，无需动态决策分支</KV>
    <KV label="vs Routing">Routing 需要根据输入选择路径；Prompt Chaining 路径固定</KV>
    <KV label="vs Orchestrator-Workers">Chaining 是线性串行；Orchestrator-Workers 是动态分解并行</KV>
    <KV label="Gate 价值">中间程序化检查防止错误向后传播——早期失败比晚期失败代价更小</KV>
  </div>
</Band>
