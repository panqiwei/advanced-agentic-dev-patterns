<Band tone="paper-raised" dense>
  <h2 class="card-title">可靠性曲面</h2>
  <p class="card-subtitle">Reliability Surface — 把单一 pass@1 拓展为三维"地形图"</p>
</Band>

<Band tone="tan">
  <CoreDef>{"pass@1 只量了血压，可靠性曲面是全身体检。三维：k（同一任务重复 k 次全部成功概率）、ε（语义等价扰动下的成功率）、λ（基础设施故障下的成功率）。曲面越平坦、越高 = 越适合生产部署。"}</CoreDef>
</Band>

<Band tone="paper-raised" grow>
  <div class="three-dims">
    <Kicker>三个维度</Kicker>
    <div class="dim-row dk">
      <span class="dim-label">k — 一致性</span>
      <span class="dim-def">同一任务重复 k 次，全部成功的概率（pass^k）</span>
      <span class="dim-insight">k=2 时即可暴露非确定性失败</span>
    </div>
    <div class="dim-row de">
      <span class="dim-label">ε — 鲁棒性</span>
      <span class="dim-def">语义等价但语法不同的扰动后成功率</span>
      <span class="dim-insight">prompt 措辞不同应该不影响结果</span>
    </div>
    <div class="dim-row dl">
      <span class="dim-label">λ — 容错性</span>
      <span class="dim-def">超时/限流/数据异常等基础设施故障下的成功率</span>
      <span class="dim-insight">生产环境不可避免的故障</span>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="empirical">
    <Kicker>ReliabilityBench 实证（1280 episodes）</Kicker>
    <div class="emp-row"><span class="emp-cond">Baseline (ε=0, λ=0)</span><span class="emp-val">96.88%</span></div>
    <div class="emp-row"><span class="emp-cond">扰动 ε=0.2</span><span class="emp-val">88.12% (-8.76pp)</span></div>
    <div class="emp-row"><span class="emp-cond">故障 λ=0.2</span><span class="emp-val">91.0% (-5.88pp)</span></div>
    <div class="emp-row emp-double"><span class="emp-cond">双重压力</span><span class="emp-val">84.0% (-12.88pp ≠ 两者之和)</span></div>
  </div>
</Band>
